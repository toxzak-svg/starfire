**Date:** 2026-04-03 | **Status:** Research Complete | **Marble 🧠**

## Top-Level Finding

The QuaNot plan is **theoretically grounded and CPU-feasible** but has some claims that need tempering:
- "Quantum-inspired" ≠ "quantum advantage" (no real quantum hardware)
- Consciousness metrics (Φ) are **intractable** for large systems — use proxy metrics
- Full AGI integration is a **multi-year project**, not 60 days
